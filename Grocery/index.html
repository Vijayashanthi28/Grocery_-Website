Frequently Asked Questions

1.) Do you offer free delivery?

Yes, we offer free standard delivery on all orders above ₹5,000. Express and scheduled delivery options are also available at checkout.

2.) How long will it take to receive my order?

Delivery times vary based on your location and the product availability. Most orders are delivered within 5–10 business days. Custom-made items may take longer.

3.) Can I return or exchange grocery after purchase?

Yes, you can return eligible items within 7 days of delivery. Items must be unused and in original condition. Please check our Return Policy for details.

4.) How can I contact customer support?

You can reach us via live chat, email at support@furnishop.com, or call our helpline at 1800-123-4567 (Mon–Sat, 10 AM – 6 PM).
